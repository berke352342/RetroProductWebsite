Walkman TPS-L2

The original Sony Walkman TPS-L2 was introduced in 1979. It changed the way people listened to music forever. Compact, portable, and with high-quality sound, it became an icon of the 80s.

  • Release Year: 1979
  • Features: Dual headphone jacks, compact design, metal casing.
  • Price: $200 (at release)
